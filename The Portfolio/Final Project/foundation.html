The Tommy Giordano Foundation

Every year the Tommy Giordano foundation hosts the annual Tommy Giordano foundation charity ball. All proceeds go to the Tommy Giordano foundation. It is always a magical night! Filled with interesting celebrated people. Nico rents out the 10th floor of the Chicago Public Library for the night and everyone stays overnight. It’s night of high stakes gambling, cream of the crop prostitutes, and live music. At the end there is a raffle too. All proceeds go to the Tommy Giordano foundation.
